Use this document to fill in the **Data Safety** section in Google Play Console.
Navigate to: Policy > App content > Data safety

---

## Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?

**Answer: No**

ShareMark does not collect, share, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Summary Statement for Play Console

When filling the Data Safety form:
1. Select **No** for "Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?"
2. Select **Yes** for "Is data encrypted in transit?" (HTTPS enforced via `usesCleartextTraffic=false`)
3. Select **Yes** for "Can users request data deletion?" (via in-app delete or uninstall)
