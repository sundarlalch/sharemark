# Privacy Policy — ShareMark

**Last updated:** April 1, 2026
**Effective date:** April 1, 2026

---

## Overview

ShareMark ("the App", "we", "our") is a bookmark manager application for Android developed by [Your Name / Company Name]. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

**Summary:** ShareMark does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data remains on your device.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data You Provide
The App allows you to save URLs (bookmarks). This data is stored **exclusively on your device** using Android's local database (Room/SQLite). It is never transmitted to any external server or third party.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data
When you save a bookmark, the App may fetch publicly available metadata (page title, description, thumbnail image) from the URL you provided. This is done via a direct request from your device to the target website — no intermediary server is involved.

The App does **not** collect:
- Your name, email, or contact information
- Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any behavioral or tracking data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

The App uses data solely to provide its features:
- Displaying your saved bookmarks
- Categorizing bookmarks using on-device machine learning
- Enabling search across your bookmark library

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All bookmark data is stored locally on your device using Android's SQLite database via the Room persistence library. The data is protected by Android's built-in app sandboxing and device encryption (if enabled on your device).

We do not operate any servers that receive or store your data.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking libraries in the App.

---

## 5. Network Access

The App requires INTERNET permission solely to:
- Fetch webpage metadata (title, description, thumbnail) when you save a URL
- Open bookmarks in your browser via Custom Tabs

The App does **not** use network access for telemetry, analytics, advertising, or any background communication.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, social networks, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

The App uses the following open-source libraries, all of which operate entirely on-device:
- **Room** (local database)
- **Glide** (image loading)
- **TensorFlow Lite** (on-device ML categorization)
- **Jsoup** (HTML parsing for metadata)

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App contains no advertising and no account creation, making it safe for general use.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:
- **View:** All data is visible within the App.
- **Delete:** You can delete individual bookmarks within the App, or uninstall the App to remove all data.
- **Export:** Data export is not currently available but may be added in a future release.
