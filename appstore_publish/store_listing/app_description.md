## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

```
ShareMark is your smart bookmark manager for Android. Save any URL from any app in one tap, and let ShareMark automatically organize it for you.

SAVE LINKS INSTANTLY
Whenever you find something worth keeping — an article, a video, a product, a recipe — just hit "Share" and choose ShareMark. Your link is saved in seconds, complete with title, thumbnail, and metadata extracted automatically.

SMART AUTO-CATEGORIZATION
Powered by on-device machine learning, ShareMark intelligently categorizes your bookmarks into:
• News & Articles
• Social Media
• Shopping
• Entertainment / Video
• Development & Tech
• Education
• Finance
• Travel
• Food & Recipes
• Sports

No manual tagging needed. ShareMark figures it out for you.

FAVORITES
Mark your most important bookmarks as favorites for instant access. Switch between All and Favorites with a single tap.

POWERFUL SEARCH
Find any saved link instantly. Search across titles, URLs, and categories.

BROWSE BY CATEGORY
Filter your entire bookmark library by category. See all your Tech reads in one place, all your Shopping links in another.

CLEAN, SIMPLE DESIGN
ShareMark uses Material Design 3 for a modern, distraction-free interface that feels native on Android.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
All your data stays on your device. ShareMark does not require an account, does not sync to any cloud, and does not collect or share any personal information. Your bookmarks are yours.

WORKS WITH EVERY APP
ShareMark integrates with your Android share sheet. If an app can share text or a URL, ShareMark can save it — browsers, social apps, news readers, email — everything works.

LIGHTWEIGHT & FAST
Built with Room database and efficient architecture for instant load times and smooth scrolling through hundreds of bookmarks.

WHY SHAREMARK?
Most bookmark apps require sign-ups, sync subscriptions, or are buried inside browsers. ShareMark is standalone, offline-first, and works across every app on your phone. It's the bookmark manager Android should have had built-in.

---
Save smarter. Browse less. Find faster.
```
